Sample Setup

General Setup Information

The setup for this game is very simple. Each player will always have one card in their hand that no other player can see. The deck of remaining cards sits at the center of all players. Next to the deck will be the stash of tokens (which come with the premium version of the game) or your own supplied tokens or coins. There is also a small rubber gun which comes with the premium version of the game. If you downloaded the print and play version of the game, then you may also use a small rubber gun, an object that is gun-shaped, or an actual gun. Once the gun is pulled from the table, it must be used, whether rubber or otherwise.

When he played the print and play version of this game while playtesting, he used a banana as the "gun." Use a firearm with live ammunition at your own risk.
