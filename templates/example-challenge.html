Challenge Chain

This is an example of a challenge.

  1. Elle claims the Athlete card.
    Diego challenges!

  2. Diego pays one coin for the challenge.

  3. Elle asks Diego if he is sure that he wants to challenge.
    Diego confirms.

  4. Elle reveals that she does have the Athlete card.
    Diego loses all of his coins.

  5. Diego plots his revenge.
    Elle decides whether to keep her Athlete or exchange. She exchanges.
