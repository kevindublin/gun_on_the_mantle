About the Game

Gun on the Mantle

Human greed is fueled by fear

You robbed a bank with several strangers from different professions, and you have all met up at the designated cabin hideout with the stash waiting. There's just one problem: the gun on the mantle. Up to twelve will enter, but only one will leave with the whole haul. Lie, cheat, and steal to eliminate others and leave with everything.


For 4-12 players, 6 recommended

Ages: 10+
