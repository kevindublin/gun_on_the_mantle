Rules

Gun

Objective & Materials

The object of the game is to be the last player remaining.

You need a stash of coins or counters and the 24 card deck that consists of four copies of six different character cards.

Each character has two abilities: one skill, one counter skill.

View Character Cards

on

Setup

Shuffle the card deck and deal each player one character card face-down and two coins.

The stash holds the remaining coins. The remaining cards are set in a draw pile.

View Sample Setup

the

Gameplay

Turns go around the table clockwise, beginning with the final player eliminated from the last game.

Each turn has four possible actions: Take a coin, Exchange a card, Use a skill from a card, Take the gun from the mantle.

Special Exception

Audible/Fair Exchange: A player cannot claim the same skill card (3) turns in a row. At the beginning of the third turn, the player is forced to either exchange before their third turn begins, then proceed with play normally or claim a take a different action.

Mantle

Challenging

A challenge can be called when any skill or counter skill is invoked and costs one coin. Any player who pays one coin may challenge.

  • If the player claiming the skill chooses not to reveal the appropriate card, they lose all of their coins and must draw a new card.
  • If the player claiming the skill does reveal the appropriate card, the challenger loses all of their coins.

View Example Challenge

Winning

The gun is used to eliminate another player. When a player is eliminated, their coins are evenly divided between the remaining players with any uneven remainder going to the killer.

When all other players are eliminated, you may take your own coins and the stash. You win.
